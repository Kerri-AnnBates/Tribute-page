Lionel Messi

The best footballer of his generation

Messi at Camp Nou stadium posing with his 4 Ballon d'Or trophies

Here's a time line of Lionel Messi's life:

  • 1987 - Born in Rosario, Argentina
  • 1993 - Joined Newell's Old Boys football club in Argentina
  • 1997 - Diagnosed with growth hormone deficiency
  • 2000 - Messi family sought to arrange a trial with Barcelona FC
  • 2001 - Messi family relocated to Barcelona
  • 2005 - Messi signed his first professional contract with Barcelona FC
  • 2007 - Became a regular starter for Barcelona Fc
  • 2008 - Won Olympic gold medal with Argentina national football team
  • 2009 - Was awarded the Fifa Ballon d'Or (best player in the world)
  • 2009 - Named Fifa world player of the year
  • 2010-2011 - Lead his team to win the Treble(win three major competitions in one season)
  • 2011 - Named UEFA Best player in Europe
  • 2015 - Won his 5th and record breaking Fifa Ballon d'Or award
  • 2016 - Copa America Top assist provider
